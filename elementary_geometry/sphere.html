Sphere

Definition: A sphere the 3d parallel of the Circle, where all points are equally distant from a center. This is applied to sphere except it is in a 3d space. The distance across from it is called diameter, and the distance from the center is called the radius.

Formulas

  • Volume: 4⁄3 πr3
  • Area: 4πr2
  • Diameter: 2r
